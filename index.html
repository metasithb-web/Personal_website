About

I’m an architecture student exploring the intersection of design, resilience, and digital craft. My work involves building systems that respond to flooding, modular housing, and community-focused spaces — all through a lens of speculative design and storytelling in both 2D, 3D, and animation.

Projects (2D & 3D)

Floating Crane Habitat (2D)

Flood-resilient vertical infrastructure across the Chao Phraya River using reused cranes and floating platforms.

Urban E-Waste Workshop (2D)

A market-workshop system on BTS bridge connections promoting sustainable e-waste recycling and community action.

Modular River Pods (3D)

Container-based living spaces stacked vertically for mobility and safety in flood-prone cities.

Cooling Tower Pavilion (3D)

A conceptual tower integrated with VRF cooling and passive environmental control systems.

Vertical Garden Structure (3D)

Public community space featuring recycled scaffolding and sustainable material reuse.

Animations

Floating Habitat Simulation: Animation showing how the flood-resilient habitat responds dynamically to water levels.

Container Market in Motion: Storyboard-style video presenting the activity and transformation of a modular container village.
